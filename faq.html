What is the Process Involved in an Internship?

Register for the Internship HR will contact you and check your profile Hr will send the payment details, You can pay an application fee of 1500INR (Indian) or 50USD (Non-Indian). Share the payment confirmation details with the HR. Once HR validates, the same will send the offer of internship. Complete the onboarding process on the day of joining the internship via online. Receive digital learning Video materials which can help to complete External certification also. On the day of internship completion, we will roll out the completion letter.

What is the work or to do?

we will assign some real time project assignments to do and candidate has to work on the same and update back with the working solution.

Do you provide any training or coaching?

We dont do any manual training or coaching like institute, but we share the learning links and video's to go through for candidates to quickly learn it.

Do you help or support during internship?

We will assign some lead to help you during internship, but they wont be doing any spoon feeding, but will give reference to check and perform self learning.

Do you offer placement or job after internship?

That is purely depends on candidate performance and job opening requirement in company only, we dont do like job assistance, we are an IT Company.

Can we come to office to do Internship?

It depends on Technology/domain to work on and its a must to spend 5-6Hrs daily Mon-Sat if want to come to office, but dont expect someone to train or teach like coaching institute, all details we specified in the application form that you read completely and submitted. Currently Web Developer, Html UI, DotNet, MVC, Marketing, HR, Finance , Laravel, Flutter can come to office to work, but its optional

Do you Issue Completion Letter?

We know fresher's or college Students find difficult to work on the Real time project assignment's. Still we provide students the learning material and the proper Completion letter, as we are Recognised as Nasscomm Member, you completion letter has more weighted advantages during your any interview with any MNC company.

Is stipend Provided?

Only for marketing we do have stipend based on Sales, for Other technology/Domain we have paused the stipend until future notified.
